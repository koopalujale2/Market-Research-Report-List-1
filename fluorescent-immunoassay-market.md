<p><p>Fluorescent immunoassay is a sensitive laboratory technique that utilizes fluorescent labels to detect specific biomolecules, such as proteins, hormones, or pathogens, in complex biological samples. This method relies on the principle of antigen-antibody binding, where a fluorescent dye is attached to an antibody. When the antibody binds to its target antigen, fluorescence is generated, which can be measured to quantify the concentration of the antigen in the sample. </p><p>The Fluorescent Immunoassay Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, the rising demand for rapid diagnostic tests, and advancements in technology. <p><p>The Fluorescent Immunoassay market is categorized primarily into ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) and rapid test kits. ELISA is a laboratory-based method utilizing enzyme-linked antibodies to detect specific antigens, offering high sensitivity and quantitative results, ideal for detailed studies. In contrast, rapid test kits provide quick results in a more user-friendly format, typically delivering qualitative results within minutes. These tests are essential in point-of-care settings, enabling immediate decision-making in clinical diagnostics and infectious disease detection.</p></p>
