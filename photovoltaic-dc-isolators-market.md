<p><strong>The Photovoltaic DC Isolators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2 Pole</li><li>4 Pole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photovoltaic DC isolators market primarily consists of 2-pole and 4-pole types. 2-pole isolators are designed for single-phase applications, typically used in residential solar systems to disconnect the solar panel strings from the inverter. Conversely, 4-pole isolators are intended for three-phase systems, commonly found in commercial and industrial solar installations, allowing for the disconnection of both positive and negative lines across multiple phases. These isolators play a crucial role in ensuring safety and compliance in solar energy applications.</p></p>
