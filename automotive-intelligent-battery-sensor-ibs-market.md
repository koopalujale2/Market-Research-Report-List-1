<p><p>The Automotive Intelligent Battery Sensor (IBS) market is divided into two main types: passive sensors and active sensors. Passive sensors monitor battery parameters like voltage and temperature without requiring additional power, making them cost-effective and energy efficient. In contrast, active sensors actively measure parameters such as current and state of charge, providing real-time data to optimize battery management. Both types enhance vehicle performance and battery lifespan, catering to the growing demand for advanced energy management in automotive applications.</p></p>
