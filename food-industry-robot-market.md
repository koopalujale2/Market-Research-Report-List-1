SCARA robots excel in pick-and-place operations due to their efficiency in horizontal motion. Parallel robots are known for high speed and load capacity. Cylindrical robots enable a combination of rotational and linear movements. Collaborative robots work alongside human workers for shared tasks, promoting safety and efficiency. Additional specialized robots meet unique industry needs.</p></p>
