<p><ul><li> Restriction Enzyme Cloning</li><li>Primer Amplification Cloning</li><li>Gene Library Cloning</li><li>Gene Mutation Cloning</li><li>Genomic Cloning </li></ul></p>
<p><p>Molecular cloning technology services encompass various methods to manipulate and replicate DNA. Restriction enzyme cloning utilizes specific enzymes to cut DNA at predefined sites, facilitating the insertion of desired genes. Primer amplification cloning involves synthesizing specific DNA primers for targeted amplification of genes. Gene library cloning creates comprehensive collections of DNA fragments for analysis. Gene mutation cloning enables the introduction of specific mutations for functional studies, while genomic cloning focuses on isolating and studying entire genomes, aiding diverse biological research applications.</p></p>
