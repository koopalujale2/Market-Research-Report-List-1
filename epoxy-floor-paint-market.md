<p><strong>The Epoxy Floor Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Epoxy Mortar Floor</li><li>Epoxy Self-Leveling Floor</li><li>Epoxy Antistatic Floor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The epoxy floor paint market includes three main types: epoxy mortar floors, epoxy self-leveling floors, and epoxy antistatic floors. Epoxy mortar floors consist of a blend of epoxy resin and aggregate, providing a durable surface suitable for heavy traffic areas. Epoxy self-leveling floors offer a smooth, seamless finish, ideal for aesthetic appeal and easy maintenance. Epoxy antistatic floors are designed to dissipate static electricity, making them essential in sensitive environments like electronics manufacturing and cleanrooms. Each type serves specific functional needs in various industries.</p></p>
