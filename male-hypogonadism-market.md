<p><p>The male hypogonadism market primarily comprises two types of therapies: Testosterone Replacement Therapy (TRT) and Gonadotropin-Releasing Hormone (GnRH) Therapy. TRT involves the administration of testosterone to alleviate symptoms related to low hormone levels, improving energy, libido, and muscle mass. In contrast, GnRH Therapy stimulates the pituitary gland to produce gonadotropins, promoting natural testosterone production. Both therapies cater to different patient needs, with TRT focusing on direct hormone replacement and GnRH promoting endogenous hormone regulation.</p></p>
