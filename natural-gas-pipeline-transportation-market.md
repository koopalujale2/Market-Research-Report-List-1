<p><strong>The Natural Gas Pipeline Transportation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gathering Pipelines</li><li>Transportation Pipelines</li><li>Distribution Pipelines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The natural gas pipeline transportation market consists of three main types: gathering pipelines, transportation pipelines, and distribution pipelines. Gathering pipelines transport gas from production sites to processing facilities. Transportation pipelines, often high-pressure systems, move processed gas over long distances to regional markets or storage facilities. Distribution pipelines deliver the gas from transportation hubs to end consumers, including homes and businesses. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring the efficient movement of natural gas through the supply chain.</p></p>
