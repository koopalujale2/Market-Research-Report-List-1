<p><p>The self-healing capacitors market is categorized into radial and axial types. Radial capacitors have leads extending from one side, facilitating easy mounting on printed circuit boards, making them suitable for compact applications. In contrast, axial capacitors feature leads on both ends, allowing for more versatile placements in various devices. Both types utilize a self-healing mechanism that enables them to recover from dielectric breakdowns, enhancing their reliability and longevity in electronic circuits, notably in power supply and filtering applications.</p></p>
