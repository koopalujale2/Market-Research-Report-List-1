<p><p>The semiconductor magnetic pumps market is categorized into two types: sealless and sealed types. Sealless magnetic pumps operate without traditional seals, minimizing leakage and facilitating maintenance, making them ideal for sensitive applications. In contrast, sealed magnetic pumps utilize mechanical seals to prevent fluid leakage, offering enhanced durability and reliability in demanding environments. Both types are crucial in semiconductor manufacturing, where precise fluid handling and contamination control are essential for maintaining high production standards and operational efficiency.</p></p>
