<p><p>The semi-elliptical leaf spring market primarily consists of two types: multi-leaf springs and mono-leaf springs. Multi-leaf springs are composed of several layered steel leaves, offering enhanced strength, load distribution, and flexibility, making them ideal for heavier vehicles. In contrast, mono-leaf springs consist of a single, thicker plate, providing a simpler design with reduced weight and improved space efficiency, suitable for lighter vehicles. Both types serve to improve vehicle stability, comfort, and performance in various applications.</p></p>
