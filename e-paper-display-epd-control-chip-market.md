Discrete controllers consist of separate components dedicated to driving EPDs, offering flexibility and customization for specific applications. In contrast, SoC combines multiple functionalities within a single chip, streamlining design and reducing space requirements. Both types cater to various applications, including e-readers, signage, and wearables, providing options based on performance, integration, and power efficiency.</p></p>
