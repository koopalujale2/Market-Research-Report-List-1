<p><strong>The Drop on Demand Inkjet Heads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Inkjet Heads</li><li>Pizeo Thin Film Inkjet Heads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Drop on Demand Inkjet Heads market comprises two primary types: Thermal Inkjet Heads and Piezo Thin Film Inkjet Heads. Thermal Inkjet Heads utilize heat to create vapor bubbles that force ink droplets through nozzles, suitable for high-resolution printing. In contrast, Piezo Thin Film Inkjet Heads employ piezoelectric materials to generate droplet ejection through mechanical movement, allowing for precise control over droplet size and placement. Both technologies cater to diverse applications in printing, packaging, and industrial sectors, enhancing print quality and efficiency.</p></p>
