<p><ul><li>GJ</li><li>LGJ</li><li>LGJF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminum conductors are categorized into three main types based on their construction and applications: GJ, LGJ, and LGJF. GJ conductors are typically used for overhead power transmission due to their lightweight and high conductivity. LGJ conductors, which include a layer of insulation, are designed for distribution lines, providing improved safety and efficiency. LGJF conductors feature a further enhanced construction with added flexibility, making them suitable for urban environments where space constraints and maneuverability are critical.</p></p>
