<p><p>The thermal scanner market comprises two primary types: fixed and portable. Fixed thermal scanners are permanently installed in locations such as airports, hospitals, and building entrances for continuous monitoring of body temperature and heat signatures. They offer high accuracy and are ideal for high-traffic areas. Portable thermal scanners, on the other hand, are lightweight and handheld, allowing for flexibility in usage across various locations. They are particularly useful for mobile screening scenarios or in areas where fixed installations are impractical.</p></p>
