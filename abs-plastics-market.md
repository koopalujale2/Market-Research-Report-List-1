<p><p>The ABS plastics market is categorized into three main types: General Grade, Flame Retardant Grade, and Others. General Grade ABS is widely used for its excellent balance of strength, toughness, and processability, making it suitable for everyday applications like consumer products. Flame Retardant Grade ABS is engineered to reduce flammability, making it ideal for electronics and automotive components where fire safety is crucial. The Others category includes specialized formulations tailored for unique applications, such as impact resistance or enhanced chemical resistance.</p></p>
