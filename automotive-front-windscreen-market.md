<p><strong>The Automotive Front Windscreen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laminated Glass</li><li>Tempered Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive front windscreen market primarily consists of laminated and tempered glass types. Laminated glass features a sandwich of two glass sheets with a laminate interlayer, enhancing safety and blocking UV rays. It minimizes shattering and protects occupants in collisions. Tempered glass, on the other hand, is heat-treated for increased strength, shattering into small pebbles upon impact, reducing injury risk. Both types meet safety standards but serve distinct needs in vehicle design and occupant protection.</p></p>
