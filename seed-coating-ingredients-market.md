<p><p>The seed coating ingredients market comprises three main types: film, encrust, and pellet. Film coatings are thin layers applied to seeds to enhance protection and provide nutrients, ensuring proper germination. Encrust coatings involve thicker applications, improving seed handling and uniformity while offering additional nutrients and pesticides. Pellet coatings create larger, round seeds that improve seeding accuracy, seed flow, and planting efficiency. Each type serves distinct agricultural needs, enhancing seed performance and ultimately supporting better crop yields.</p></p>
