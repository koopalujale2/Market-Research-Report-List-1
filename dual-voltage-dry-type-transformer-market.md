<p><ul><li>Commutator Type</li><li>Rectifier Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dual voltage dry type transformer market is segmented into commutator and rectifier types. Commutator type transformers utilize mechanical switches to alternate between different voltage levels, offering flexibility in applications. Rectifier type transformers, on the other hand, convert alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC) while allowing dual voltage outputs, making them ideal for various industrial settings. Both types emphasize safety and efficiency, catering to diverse sectors that require reliable power management solutions.</p></p>
