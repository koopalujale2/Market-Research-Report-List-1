MLPA enables the detection of genomic alterations through targeted probes, facilitating the analysis of multiple genes simultaneously. Fragment Analysis PCR provides precise sizing of PCR products, aiding in detecting genetic anomalies. Together, these methods enhance the understanding and diagnosis of neurogenetic disorders.</p></p>
