<p><ul><li>Planar Target</li><li>Rotary Target</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor ceramic target market is primarily divided into two types: planar targets and rotary targets. Planar targets are flat and used in applications requiring uniform deposition over large areas, making them suitable for thin-film coatings. Rotary targets, on the other hand, are cylindrical and designed for continuous material deposition, enhancing efficiency and reducing waste during the sputtering process. Both types play crucial roles in semiconductor manufacturing, catering to varying production needs and technologies.</p></p>
