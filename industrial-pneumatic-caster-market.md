<p><p>The Industrial Pneumatic Caster market comprises two primary types: rigid casters and swivel casters. Rigid casters are fixed in position, allowing for straight-line movement and stability, making them ideal for heavier loads and specific paths. In contrast, swivel casters can rotate 360 degrees, offering greater maneuverability for navigating tight spaces and changing directions easily. Both types are essential in industrial settings, facilitating the efficient movement of equipment and goods in warehouses, factories, and manufacturing plants.</p></p>
