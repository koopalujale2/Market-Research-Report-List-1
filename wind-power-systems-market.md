<p><p>Wind power systems are categorized into on-grid and off-grid markets. On-grid systems are connected to the electric grid, allowing excess energy to be fed back for utility use, benefiting from net metering and offering reliable power supply. Off-grid systems, on the other hand, operate independently, providing energy in remote areas without grid access. These systems often integrate battery storage to ensure a continuous power supply, catering to specific energy needs while enhancing energy independence and sustainability.</p></p>
