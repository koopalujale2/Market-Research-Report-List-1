<p><p>The Visual SLAM Autonomous Mobile Robot market is categorized into two main types: Monocular Vision and Binocular Vision. Monocular Vision utilizes a single camera to perceive the environment, relying on image processing techniques to estimate depth and map surroundings. In contrast, Binocular Vision employs two cameras, mimicking human depth perception by analyzing disparity between images to achieve more accurate 3D mapping. Each type caters to different applications, with monocular systems being cost-effective and simpler, while binocular systems offer enhanced accuracy and robustness.</p></p>
