<p><strong>The Microbial Enhanced Oil Recovery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ground Method</li><li>Reservoir Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microbial Enhanced Oil Recovery (MEOR) involves utilizing microbes to improve oil extraction efficiency. The Ground Method focuses on introducing algae or bacteria near the surface to increase oil mobility, enhancing the dislodging of stuck oil. In contrast, the Reservoir Method targets deeper formations, injecting tailored microbial solutions to alter reservoir conditions, reduce viscosity, and improve oil flow. Both methods aim to optimize oil recovery while potentially lowering environmental impact compared to traditional techniques, showcasing their distinct applications in the oil industry.</p></p>
