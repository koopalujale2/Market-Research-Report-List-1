<p><strong>The Polypropylene Geotextile Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Woven</li><li>Woven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene geotextile market comprises two primary types: non-woven and woven. Non-woven geotextiles are made from randomly oriented fibers, providing excellent filtration, drainage, and separation properties. They are commonly used in erosion control and soil stabilization. Woven geotextiles, on the other hand, consist of interwoven fibers, offering higher tensile strength and durability, making them ideal for reinforcement and load distribution in construction applications. Both types play crucial roles in civil engineering and environmental projects.</p></p>
