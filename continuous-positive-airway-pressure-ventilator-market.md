<p><strong>The Continuous Positive Airway Pressure Ventilator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed-CPAP</li><li>Auto CPAP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) ventilator market includes two primary types: Fixed CPAP and Auto CPAP. Fixed CPAP delivers a constant air pressure throughout the night, providing a steady airflow to keep airways open, making it suitable for users with stable sleep apnea. In contrast, Auto CPAP adjusts the air pressure automatically based on the user's needs, responding to changes in airflow resistance during sleep, which enhances comfort and efficacy for users with varying apnea severity.</p></p>
