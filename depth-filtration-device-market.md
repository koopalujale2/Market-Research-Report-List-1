<p><strong>The Depth Filtration Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bottom-In–Top-Out Mode (Inline Configuration)</li><li>Bottom-In–Bottom-Out Mode (T-Configuration)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The depth filtration device market features two key configurations: the Bottom-In–Top-Out mode (Inline Configuration) and the Bottom-In–Bottom-Out mode (T-Configuration). In the Inline Configuration, fluid enters from the bottom and exits from the top, allowing for continuous flow and efficient filtration in processes like water treatment. Meanwhile, the T-Configuration allows fluid to enter from the bottom and exit from the bottom, facilitating greater compatibility with various system designs and enhancing space efficiency in applications like pharmaceuticals and food processing.</p></p>
