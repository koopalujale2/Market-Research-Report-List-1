<p><strong>The PC VR Headsets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3DOF Motion Tracking Type</li><li>6DOF Motion Tracking Type</li><li>9DOF Motion Tracking Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PC VR headset market can be categorized into three main types based on motion tracking capabilities. </p><p>The 3DOF (Three Degrees of Freedom) headsets track rotational movement, allowing users to look around but not physically move within a space. </p><p>6DOF (Six Degrees of Freedom) headsets add positional tracking, enabling users to move in and out of virtual environments. </p><p>The 9DOF (Nine Degrees of Freedom) headsets include additional sensors, offering enhanced spatial awareness and more fluid interactions in VR experiences.</p></p>
