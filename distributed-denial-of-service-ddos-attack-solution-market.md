<p><p>The Distributed Denial-of-Service (DDoS) attack solution market encompasses various attack types, including UDP Flood, ICMP Flood, SYN Flood, and HTTP Flood. UDP Flood targets network bandwidth by overwhelming it with packets, while ICMP Flood exploits the Internet Control Message Protocol to create excessive traffic. SYN Flood targets the connection process of TCP, leading to resource exhaustion. HTTP Flood focuses on overloading web servers with legitimate-looking requests. Other market solutions address diverse attack methods and emerging threats in cybersecurity.</p></p>
