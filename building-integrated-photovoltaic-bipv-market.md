<p><p>Building Integrated Photovoltaics (BIPV) are solar energy systems integrated into building materials, such as roofs and facades, enhancing aesthetics while generating electricity. The market is primarily divided into two types: Crystalline Silicon and Thin Film. Crystalline Silicon BIPV offers high efficiency and durability, making it suitable for various architectural applications. In contrast, Thin Film BIPV is lightweight and flexible, allowing for easier integration into unconventional surfaces, although it typically has lower efficiency than crystalline options. Together, they support sustainable building designs.</p></p>
