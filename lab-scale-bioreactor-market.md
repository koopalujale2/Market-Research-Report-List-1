<p><p>The lab scale bioreactor market comprises three main types: glass, stainless steel, and single-use plastic polymer. Glass bioreactors are favored for their visibility and resistance to chemical reactions, making them ideal for small-scale experiments. Stainless steel bioreactors offer durability and are suitable for high-pressure processes, often used in more rigorous applications. Single-use plastic polymer bioreactors have gained popularity due to their convenience, reduced contamination risk, and lower maintenance costs, facilitating efficient biotech research and development.</p></p>
