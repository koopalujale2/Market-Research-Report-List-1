<p><p>The aluminum alloy casting market comprises several key types, including die casting, permanent mold casting, and others. Die casting involves forcing molten aluminum into a mold under high pressure, resulting in precise and complex shapes ideal for high-volume production. Permanent mold casting uses reusable molds to produce durable parts with smoother surfaces and better dimensional accuracy. Other methods, such as sand casting and investment casting, cater to specialized needs and lower production volumes, offering diverse solutions for various applications.</p></p>
