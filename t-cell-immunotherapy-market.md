<p><p>The T-cell immunotherapy market includes several advanced treatments targeting cancer. CAR-T therapy involves engineering a patient's T-cells to express chimeric antigen receptors, allowing them to identify and attack cancer cells. TCR therapy focuses on modifying T-cells to recognize specific tumor antigens via T-cell receptors. TIL therapy uses tumor-infiltrating lymphocytes extracted from a patient’s tumor, which are then expanded and reinfused to enhance anti-tumor response. These therapies represent innovative approaches to harnessing the immune system against cancer.</p></p>
