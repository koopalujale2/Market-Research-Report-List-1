<p><p>The advanced drug delivery market encompasses various types designed to enhance therapeutic efficacy. Polymeric drug delivery systems utilize biocompatible polymers for controlled release. Implants and intrauterine devices (IUDs) provide localized and prolonged medication, particularly in reproductive health. Sustained release formulations maintain drug levels over time, improving adherence. Targeted drug delivery systems aim to direct therapies to specific sites, minimizing systemic effects. Prodrugs are inactive compounds that convert into active drugs in the body, optimizing absorption and distribution.</p></p>
