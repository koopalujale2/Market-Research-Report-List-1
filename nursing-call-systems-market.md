<p><strong>The Nursing Call Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wired Nursing Call Systems</li><li>Wireless Nursing Call Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nursing call systems are essential in healthcare for facilitating communication between patients and staff. Wired nursing call systems rely on physical cabling to connect devices, offering reliability and stable connectivity but limited flexibility in reconfiguration. In contrast, wireless nursing call systems utilize radio signals for communication, allowing greater mobility and easier installation while reducing infrastructure costs. Each type serves unique needs; wired systems are ideal for fixed environments, while wireless options are suited for dynamic healthcare settings.</p></p>
