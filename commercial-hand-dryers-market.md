<p><p>The commercial hand dryers market is primarily divided into two types: jet air hand dryers and hot air hand dryers. Jet air hand dryers use high-velocity jets of air to quickly remove moisture from hands, offering fast drying times and energy efficiency. In contrast, hot air hand dryers rely on heated air to evaporate water, providing a gentler drying experience but typically taking longer. Each type caters to different user preferences and settings, influencing their adoption in commercial spaces.</p></p>
