<p><p>Rubber track pads for excavators come in three main types: Bolt-On, Clip-On, and Chain-On. Bolt-On type pads are securely attached with bolts, ensuring stability and durability for heavy-duty applications. Clip-On pads are easily installed and removed, offering versatility for varying terrain while providing a snug fit. Chain-On pads are designed to be mounted onto the excavator's undercarriage chains, delivering excellent traction and protection. Each type serves distinct requirements, enhancing performance and efficiency in construction and excavation tasks.</p></p>
