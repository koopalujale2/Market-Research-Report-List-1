<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Programmatic Display Advertising Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Real Time Bidding</li><li>Private Marketplace</li><li>Automated Guaranteed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Programmatic display advertising encompasses three main market types: </p><p>1. **Real-Time Bidding (RTB)** allows advertisers to bid on ad inventory in real-time, ensuring the best price and targeting based on user data and behavior.</p><p>2. **Private Marketplace (PMP)** is an invitation-only marketplace where premium publishers offer their inventory to select advertisers, providing higher quality placements without the competitiveness of open auctions.</p><p>3. **Automated Guaranteed** involves pre-negotiated deals between advertisers and publishers, ensuring fixed pricing and inventory, enhancing predictability and reliability in ad buying.</p></p>
