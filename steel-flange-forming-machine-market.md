<p><strong>The Steel Flange Forming Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Simplex</li><li>Duplex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel flange forming machine market is primarily categorized into simplex and duplex types. Simplex machines operate on a single forming station, making them suitable for lower-volume production with less complex designs. In contrast, duplex machines feature dual forming stations, allowing for increased efficiency and higher output by processing two flanges simultaneously. This differentiation caters to various manufacturing needs, with simplex machines ideal for small-scale operations and duplex machines preferred for larger production demands and more intricate flange shapes.</p></p>
