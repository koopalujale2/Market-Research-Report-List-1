<p><p>The Plasma Surface Treatment System market is divided into two primary types: Atmospheric Pressure Type and Low Pressure/Vacuum Plasma Type. Atmospheric Pressure Type systems operate at ambient conditions, allowing for direct treatment of surfaces without needing a vacuum, thus facilitating larger-scale applications and rapid processing. In contrast, Low Pressure/Vacuum Plasma Type systems operate in controlled environments where reduced pressure enhances uniformity and effectiveness, making them suitable for intricate or delicate materials, often used in specialized industries like electronics and medical devices.</p></p>
