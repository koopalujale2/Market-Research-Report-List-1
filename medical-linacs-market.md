<p><p>The medical LINAC (Linear Accelerator) market is categorized by energy levels, which determine their applications in radiation therapy. Low Energy LINACs (4-8 MeV) are primarily used for superficial tumors, providing targeted treatment with minimal penetration. Medium Energy LINACs (5-15 MeV) are more versatile, suitable for treating intermediate-depth tumors. High Energy LINACs (18-25 MeV) are designed for deep-seated tumors, delivering significant doses to target and spare surrounding tissues. Each type plays a crucial role in precision cancer treatment, optimizing patient outcomes based on tumor characteristics.</p></p>
