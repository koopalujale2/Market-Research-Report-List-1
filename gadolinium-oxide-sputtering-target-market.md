<p><p>The Gadolinium Oxide Sputtering Target market is categorized into two primary types: Rotatable Transformation and Non-Rotating Type. Rotatable Transformation targets allow for continuous rotation during the sputtering process, resulting in a uniform deposition over a larger area, enhancing efficiency and target utilization. In contrast, Non-Rotating Type targets are stationary, often used for smaller-scale applications or specific designs where a fixed positioning is preferred. Each type serves distinct needs based on deposition requirements, substrate size, and desired film characteristics.</p></p>
